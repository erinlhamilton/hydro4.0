About

The Fox-Wolf Hydrologic Dashboard is a tool to visualize how storm events move through the Fox-Wolf watershed in northeastern Wisconsin and into the receiving water body of Green Bay. It does this through linking together storm total precipitation data from 2003 to present with NWIS stream gauge precipitation, streamflow, and catchment area data. The future vision of the Hydrologic Dashboard is to combine many sources of data into one location to further aid in linking how storm events over the Fox-Wolf watershed influence Green Bay.

Data Sources:

Stream Gauges: USGS National Water Information System (NWIS)

Storm Events: University of Wisconsin Sea Grant Institute

Green Bay Water Overlays: Michigan Tech Research Institute (MTRI)

Mapping API: Leaflet

Mapping Tiles: Open Streetmap
